# Glossary

*Last updated: 2026-07-08.*

This glossary defines every term of art used in the Manatee design docs, for two audiences at once: **programmers who are not electrical engineers**, and **electrical engineers who are not programmers**. Every entry opens with a plain-language sentence, explains the concept with an analogy from the *other* domain, states the standard-literature name where our usage is project-coined or nonstandard, points to where the term appears in the docs, and cites real literature where a citation earns its place.

Entries were drafted against the actual doc sections where each term is used, then adversarially fact-checked (technical accuracy, fidelity to project usage, citation authenticity, dual-audience readability) and revised. If an entry contradicts a design doc, the doc wins — file it as a glossary bug.

The entries live in category files under [`glossary/`](glossary/):

- [Circuits & Electrical Engineering](glossary/circuits.md) — Electrical concepts for readers coming from software: components, laws, AC/DC behavior, hazards. (228 entries)
- [Numerics & Solver Mathematics](glossary/numerics.md) — How the math engine works: MNA, integration, factorization, convergence — explained in systems terms. (78 entries)
- [Project-Coined Terms (part 1)](glossary/project-1.md) — Manatee-specific vocabulary, each mapped to the closest standard concept in the literature. (174 entries)
- [Project-Coined Terms (part 2)](glossary/project-2.md) — Manatee-specific vocabulary, each mapped to the closest standard concept in the literature. (169 entries)
- [Programming & Software](glossary/programming.md) — Software concepts for readers coming from electrical engineering: the vocabulary the design docs assume. (236 entries)
- [Game Integration](glossary/games.md) — Vintage Story and Stationeers concepts: engine facts, modding seams, game-side vocabulary. (47 entries)
